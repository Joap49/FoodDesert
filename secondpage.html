How can we help?

1. Find local food deserts.

To find a food desert near you, enter your zip code in the USDA’s Food Desert Locator: ers.usda.gov/data/fooddesert.

2. Volunteer at community gardens. The American Community Gardening Association provides lists and maps of community gardens, so you can locate those near food deserts that may donate fresh food to neighbors in need: communitygarden.org/find-a-garden.

3. Donate fresh produce. Find food banks, soup kitchens, and other organizations fighting hunger through Food Pantries or Feeding America: foodpantries.org feedingamerica.org/find-your- local-foodbank.
